About Us

In 2017 group of three friends Amit Singh, Amarjeet Singh and Krishna Singh started Singh Brother Educare a Coaching Institute with Mission of Revolutionizing Education and transforming lives. Prior to that ,they are practicing in Private firms. Our vision is to become an Renowned premier Organization in field of education . To provide State-of-the-Art Infrastructure and right academic ambience for developing professional skills as well as an environment for growth of leadership and managerial skills to students which will make them competent student to deliver quality results in their academics At SBE ,the importance of faculty is well understood and is reflected in qualified teaching staff .A closely monitored mechanism ensures proper coverage of syllabus within right time of frame. The institute is established in academic year 2017-18 with a clear objective of providing quality education with standard requirement.
